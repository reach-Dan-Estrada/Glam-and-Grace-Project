SERVICES

Our team at Salon has an inclusive mindset which welcomes and embraces diversity. Our ultimate goal is to make every client feel safe, valued, and appreciated while also celebrating their individualism. Self-expression is, at its core, the action of expressing yourself through creative activities.

Two Tone w/ Keratin Blowdry

Two Tone hair color paired with a Keratin Blowdry treatment can leave your locks looking sleek and shiny with minimal frizz.

Ash Highlights

The subtle, ashy tones of these highlights can provide a natural-looking contrast and dimension, without overpowering your base hair color.

Two Toned Balayage

Two Toned Balayage is a versatile hair color technique that blends two complementary shades together for a seamless, natural-looking transition.

One Toned Color

Opting for a One Toned Color can provide a classic and timeless look that is easy to maintain.

Color w/ Keratin Blowdry

The combination of color and Keratin Blowdry can add dimension and shine to your hair, while reducing frizz and flyaways, resulting in a silky and glossy finish.

Toned Purple Ends

Toned purple ends is a hair coloring technique that involves using a purple toner to dye the tips or ends of the hair in a subtle or bold shade of purple.
